**Arata Amemiya** is a research scientist at RIKEN Center for Interdisciplinary Theoretical and Mathematical Sciences (iTHEMS) and Center for Computational Science (R-CCS), Kobe, Japan. His research interests include weather and climate dynamics, atmospheric modeling, and theories and application of data assimilation. He is mainly working on [PREVENIR](https://sites.google.com/view/prevenir-jp/home), a Japan-Argentina collaborative project under [JICA/JST SATREPS](https://www.jst.go.jp/global/) program. He is also a developer and maintainer of the [SCALE-LETKF](https://github.com/SCALE-LETKF-RIKEN/scale-letkf), a regional data assimilation and numerical weather prediction system.
